Glossary

Key terms and acronyms used throughout the Superfeet eCommerce platform documentation.

A

Apps

Third-party applications installed on Shopify stores to extend functionality. Superfeet uses apps for search, email marketing, reviews, and other features.

Athos Commerce

Rebranded name for SearchSpring, the enterprise search and merchandising platform used across all Superfeet stores.

C

Calibreapp

Performance monitoring tool that tracks Core Web Vitals and page load times across the Superfeet platform.

Collections

Groups of products organized by category, type, or other criteria. Can be manual (hand-picked) or smart (rule-based).

COGS

Cost of Goods Sold - the direct costs associated with producing products, tracked in Shopify for profitability analysis.

CQL Propel

The custom Shopify theme (v24.3.0) used across all Superfeet stores, built by CQL Corp on the Dawn theme foundation.

CRO

Conversion Rate Optimization - the process of improving the percentage of visitors who complete desired actions on the website.

CWV

Core Web Vitals - Google's metrics for measuring user experience, including loading performance, interactivity, and visual stability.

D

Dawn

Shopify's reference theme that serves as the foundation for the CQL Propel theme used by Superfeet.

E

Elevar

Server-side conversion tracking platform that bypasses ad blockers and iOS restrictions to provide accurate attribution data.

G

GA4

Google Analytics 4 - the latest version of Google's analytics platform used to track user behavior and conversions.

Gorgias

Customer service platform that integrates with Shopify to provide unified customer support across all channels.

I

Insole Finder

Custom-built product recommendation quiz that guides customers to the right insole based on foot type and activity preferences.

K

Klaviyo

Email and SMS marketing automation platform used for customer segmentation, abandoned cart recovery, and lifecycle campaigns.

L

Launchpad

Shopify app that enables scheduled publishing of products, collections, and other content changes.

Liquid

Shopify's templating language used to create dynamic content in themes, sections, and snippets.

M

Matrixify

Bulk data management app used for importing/exporting products, customers, orders, and inventory across all regional stores.

Metafields

Custom data fields that extend Shopify's default product, variant, and page data. Used extensively in the CQL Propel theme for product attributes.

O

Osano

Cookie consent management platform that handles GDPR and CCPA compliance across all Superfeet stores.

P

PDP

Product Detail Page - the individual product page where customers view product information, images, and purchase options.

PLP

Product Listing Page - pages that display multiple products, such as collection pages or search results.

R

ReCharge

Subscription management platform that handles recurring orders and customer subscription management.

RX Microsite

Specialized section of the website for healthcare professionals and prescription orthotics, identified by the cql.rx_microsite metafield.

S

SearchSpring

Enterprise search and merchandising platform (rebranding to Athos Commerce) that replaces Shopify's native search functionality.

Sections

Reusable page components in Shopify themes that can be added to any page template. The CQL Propel theme includes 105+ sections.

SKU

Stock Keeping Unit - unique identifier for each product variant used for inventory management and order processing.

Snippets

Reusable code components in Shopify themes that can be included in multiple sections. The CQL Propel theme includes 143 snippets.

T

Theme Customizer

Shopify's visual editor that allows non-technical users to customize theme settings, colors, fonts, and layout without coding.

TTI

Time to Interactive - a Core Web Vital metric that measures how long it takes for a page to become fully interactive.

V

Variants

Different versions of a product (e.g., different sizes, colors, or styles) that share the same base product information.

Y

Yotpo

Customer review and user-generated content platform that collects and displays product reviews across all Superfeet stores.
